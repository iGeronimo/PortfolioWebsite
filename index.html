About me

I am a Unity Developer currently located in Wrocław, Poland. Playing football for 16 years and developing games at OAMK, Gamious and Landell Games has taught me to be a team‑oriented individual.

At Gamious I contributed to an RTS and later moved to a larger team to help with a wordplay game, where I focused on adaptive UI across platforms.
In my spare time I’m an avid gamer and keyboard enthusiast. I love customizing peripherals and experimenting with new tools.

I’m a curious builder—whenever something piques my interest, I dig deep. This site was made without frameworks to learn more about HTML, CSS, and JS. View the source code.
